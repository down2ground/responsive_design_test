Paragraphs and horizontal lines

Paragraphs and horizontal lines must not overflow the page right margin when the page resizes. Also their width should not grow over the specified limit.

Fenced blocks may be inserted inside other blocks like blockquotes:

$ echo Fenced blocks inside a blockquote
Fenced blocks inside a blockquote
